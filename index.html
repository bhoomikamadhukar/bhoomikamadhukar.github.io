Reinforcement Learning

Virtual Self Driving Car

This is my effort to use Reinforcement learning to build a virtual self driving car. The agent, which is a car in this case learns in an environment, which is a map, and through the long process of trial and error uses feedback from the model and learns to drive only along the road. The algorithm used is T3D model with CNN. To make the model understand the difference between the road and the buildings, I created a mask where the roads are highlighted in black and overlapped my image on the mask.
